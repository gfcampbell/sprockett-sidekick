Report Issues

Help us maintain a safe and productive platform by reporting:

  • Inappropriate AI suggestions: Harmful, biased, or inappropriate coaching recommendations
  • Technical bugs: Software issues, unexpected behavior, security concerns
  • Misuse of platform: Violations of our terms of service
  • Privacy concerns: Data handling or privacy-related issues

Report to: report@sprockett.ai

Please include as much detail as possible, including screenshots or recordings if relevant and appropriate.

  • Cybercrime: FBI Internet Crime Complaint Center
  • Child safety: NCMEC CyberTipline
