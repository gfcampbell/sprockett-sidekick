Privacy Policy

How Sprockett protects your conversations while providing AI-powered coaching during your calls.

Your conversations stay private. Sprockett uses end-to-end encryption for all calls, processes AI suggestions locally when possible, and never stores call recordings or transcripts without your explicit consent.

Effective Date: January 15, 2025

How Smart Calls Work

Sprockett provides AI-powered conversation coaching during your video calls. Here's how we protect your privacy while delivering intelligent suggestions:

Call Privacy

  • End-to-end encryption: All video and audio is encrypted using WebRTC DTLS-SRTP
  • No call storage: We never store, record, or retain your video calls
  • Temporary processing: Audio is processed only for real-time AI suggestions
  • Local transcription: When possible, speech-to-text happens on your device

AI Processing

  • Real-time only: AI suggestions are generated in real-time and not stored
  • No conversation logs: We don't keep records of what was said during calls
  • Encrypted transmission: Any data sent for AI processing is encrypted in transit
  • Prompt privacy: Your custom AI prompts are processed securely and not stored

What Data We Collect

Account Information (Optional)

  • Email address (if you choose to create an account for saved preferences)
  • Custom AI prompts (if you save them for future use)
  • Usage preferences and settings

Technical Data

  • Connection metadata for call quality (no content)
  • Aggregate usage statistics (anonymized)
  • Error logs for service improvement (no personal data)

What We Don't Collect

  • Call recordings or audio files
  • Conversation transcripts (unless you explicitly save them)
  • Personal conversations or meeting content
  • Participant identities or contact information

AI and Third-Party Services

For AI-powered suggestions, we may use secure, privacy-focused AI services. When this happens:

  • Data is transmitted with encryption and processed securely
  • No conversation content is stored by AI providers
  • We only work with providers who meet our privacy standards
  • You can opt out of AI features entirely if preferred

Your Control

  • AI toggle: Enable or disable AI assistance at any time
  • Custom prompts: Choose what guidance the AI provides
  • No account required: Use Sprockett without any registration
  • Delete anytime: Remove any saved preferences or prompts

Contact & Updates

Questions about privacy? Contact us at privacy@sprockett.ai

We'll update this policy as needed and notify users of significant changes.
